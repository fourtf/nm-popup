Usage: nm-popup [qt-options] [options]
Qt-Options:
  -style [style]       Selects a Qt style.
  -stylesheet [path]   Loads a Qss Stylesheet for customizing the look of the
                       Application, works similar to css.
  (more Qt specific options may be available)
Options:
  --duplicates         Show duplicate SSID's.
  --preserve-style     Disables the default theme modifications which make the
                       ui darker.
